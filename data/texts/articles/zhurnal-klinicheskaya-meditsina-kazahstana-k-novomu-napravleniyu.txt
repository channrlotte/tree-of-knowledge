Our journal is now indexing in databases - Global Impact Factor, Open Academic Journals Index and International Impact Factor Services.
These are one of the largest databases where conducted in-depth quantitative and qualitative analysis to assess the prestige periodical scientific publications in the world. The rating takes into account such factors as peer-review the original article, the quality of scientific and technical review, acceptance and rejection rate of the manuscripts and the periodicity of the publication numbers.
In general, the stringent requirements for the selection of articles, high rejection rate of manuscripts are considered one of the important criteria of high peer review quality and prestige of the journal. Our impact factor in the system of Global Impact Factor is 0.542.
